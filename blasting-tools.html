Overview of blasting tools and materials I use for surface preparation works.

Automatic Blasting Machine

Wheel / automatic blasting machine for structural steel, pipes and plates in workshop.

  • Continuous conveyor / roller type or hanger type
  • Used for mass production and uniform surface preparation

Manual Blasting Booth (Indoor)

Enclosed blast room with dust extraction and recovery system for manual blasting.

  • Blast pot, nozzle, deadman handle
  • Abrasive recycling & dust collector
  • Lighting and ventilation system

Manual Blasting Setup for Outside Project

Portable field setup used for site structures, tanks, pipe racks and maintenance jobs.

  • Diesel / electric air compressor
  • Portable blast pot with moisture separator
  • Blast hose, air hose & couplings
  • Deadman switch & nozzle holder

Abrasive & Consumables

Different abrasives selected as per client specification & surface profile requirement.

  • Copper slag / coal slag
  • Garnet (different mesh sizes)
  • Steel grit / steel shot (for wheel blasting)
  • Masking tapes & covering materials

Blasting PPE & Breathing System

Operator safety for continuous blasting operation.

  • Blasting helmet & cape
  • Fresh air breathing system with filter
  • Ear protection, gloves & safety boots
  • Blast suit / apron

Blasting Control & Monitoring

Support tools used to control air & pressure during blasting.

  • Blasting pressure gauge at pot outlet
  • Moisture trap & air filter
  • Whip check & safety clips
